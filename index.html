Curriculum: Accounting Fundamentals

Chapter 1: Accounting Basics

Accounting conventions, types of accounts, double entry system.

Chapter 2: Journal & Ledger

Journal entries, ledger posting, trial balance preparation.

Chapter 3: Final Accounts

Trading account, P&L account, balance sheet preparation.

Chapter 4: Bank Reconciliation

BRS preparation, reconciliation statements.

Chapter 5: Cloud Accounting

AI-based cloud accounting fundamentals.

Curriculum: GST Mastery

GST Fundamentals

History, CGST/SGST/IGST, definitions, scope of supply.

GST Practical on Tally

GST setup in Tally, voucher entries, tax calculations.

GST Returns

GSTR-1, GSTR-2, GSTR-3B filing on portal.

Advanced GST

Input tax credit, e-way bill, e-invoice, export/import.

GST Compliance

Tax payments, penalties, audits, assessments.

Income Tax Basics

Heads of income: Salary, House Property, Business, Capital Gains.

ITR Filing

ITR 1 to ITR 4 preparation and online filing.

TDS Fundamentals

TDS sections, rate limits, due dates, Form 16.

TDS Compliance

TDS returns, payments, interest, and penalties.

Advanced Topics

Advance tax, tax audit, computation of total income.
